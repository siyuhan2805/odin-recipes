Lemonade Recipe

Description

Got lemons? You know what they say...this sweet lemonade is just whats needed to quench summer thirsts.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups caster sugar
  • 1.25 litres lemon juice
  • 2 litres cold water
  • Crush ice, to serve
  • 1 lemon sliced, to serve

Steps

  1. Place sugar, lemon juice and water in a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook, stirring, for 15 minutes or until sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat. Set aside to cool for 20 minutes.
  2. Transfer to heatproof jugs. Cover, then refrigerate overnight.
  3. Add crushed ice and lemon slices to lemonade. Serve. s
